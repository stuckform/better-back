About Me

Hello! I am Nogui Solano your certified personal trainer with a focus on functional full body health. Here are some of my core values, I am passionate about guiding you to your true healthy and happy self.

Strong back, strong future

Most people chase fitness goals like flat abs or defined muscles, focusing on what they see in the mirror. But true optimal health goes beyond aesthetics. It’s about building a powerhouse foundation—starting with your back. A strong back is the key to unlocking your potential, driving you forward, and protecting your body from the inside out. It’s not just about looking good; it’s about moving better, feeling stronger, and creating a future where you stand taller and live pain-free. Prioritize your back, and the rest will follow.

Foundational Fitness

It’s time to redefine what it means to pursue optimal health and a strong physique. Forget the quick fixes and extreme workouts that promise results but leave you burned out. True fitness is about building a solid foundation that empowers you to break through your limitations. Together, we’ll focus on sustainable, personalized strategies that strengthen you from the inside out. I’ll work with you to create a strong, reliable base for your body—one that supports your goals and propels you forward with confidence. This is more than just training; it’s crafting a lasting legacy of health and resilience.
